Located in vibrant Kaloor, Kochi, AL ARBANIA isn't just a restaurant—it's a celebration of bold flavors. We bring together the slow-roasted, tender perfection of Middle Eastern meats and the fast-paced, savory umami of authentic Chinese cooking under one premium roof.

Every dish is crafted with passion, using hand-picked ingredients to ensure every bite is a masterpiece. Come hungry, leave mesmerized.
